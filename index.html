Discover Good Eats
//off the eaten path//

"So (insert name here), where's a good place to eat?"

How many times have you been ask this question? Sure, one could point out the usual tourist traps but you don't want to be that friend. This is YOUR hood! You rep this city and you want to show them the hidden gems. The non-discriptive joint where locals go. Whether it's a taco truck posted up in the gas station parking lot, the no-frills burger joint with no seating, or the "best" fried chicken in town located inside of a gas station, you know where the spots are.

My hope is to provide you, the visiting hungry masses, my curated list of good eats around Charlotte, NC and surrounding areas. I've also enlisted a few foodie friends to provide a list of their go-to for out of town visitors.

Follow me on my adventures OFF THE EATEN PATH.
